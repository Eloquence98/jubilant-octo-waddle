FAQ

What is an NFT?

A non-fungible token ( NFT ) is a unique digital asset that represents ownership of real-world items like art, video clips, music, and more. NFTs use the same blockchain technology that powers cryptocurrencies. If you want to know more about NFTs, check out some great Youtube videos or message us.

Where can I buy your NFTs?

You can buy them easily on OpenSea, the biggest platform made for NFTs. If you need any assistance, just let us know, we will help you.

Who created the Baby Ape Boat Club collection?

The only creator, founder and current CEO is Skurvydogg. He is the one who created, minted and listed all 333 NFTs.

How many BABC NFTs are there? Will you create more?

No, there is (and will ever be) only 333 Baby Apes Boat Club collection NFTs in the world. Join the 0.00000004% of population in the world and become a BABC owner.

We have a lot of new huge changes, plans, events and partnerships for 2022. We want to focus on kids such as charities and entertaiment like TV shows, comic books, toys and games.
